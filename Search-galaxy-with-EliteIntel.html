Searching

Find a Mining Site

A useful feature for space-miners. Find where I can mine some Low Temperature Diamonds within X light years The app will query Spansh for a knokwn location of a star system that contains a ring planet with deposits of the material specified. Especially useful when you and your Fleet Carrier is far out in the black. You do not have to stick to Tritium highway. Other explorers have reported many Tritium sites all over the galaxy and you will find one within one or two carrier jumps from your location with a high degree of probability.

Finding a Commodity

To locate a specific commodity, use the following process:

Request a specific commodity search:

Speak naturally to EliteIntel. For example, Where can I buy Tritium? or Find a market that has Bromellite. EliteIntel queries crowd data for the best market prices within X light years of your current position. When results are found, it will plot a route to the best match. You can refine your queries by asking for the cheapest or closest and provide range. For example: Find a market where we can buy gold within 50 light years, go for the cheapest one

NOTE: The market data is time-sensitive. If no commanders running EDMC passed through a station, neither EDSM nor Spansh will have up-to-date information. Even if you see it in INARA. INARA does not have an API for apps to query it, so we can't get info from there.

Find a broker / trader

Ask the app Find a raw/manufactured/encoded material trader within X light years. It will search Spansh and plot a route to the nearest star system where a material trader is located, according to data in Spansh.

You can ask Locate Guardian/Human technology broker within X light years. If you do not indicate the range it will search within about 100 ly from your location. (subject to change in the future to X times your ship range)

NOTE: Same as above. The data we get can be aged. The app will tell you how long ago the data was updated, and if the result is a month ago, perhaps that system may not have the broker you are looking for anymore.
